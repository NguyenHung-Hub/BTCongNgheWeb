A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mauth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smile include a contraction of the muscles at the comer of the eyes, an action know as a "Duchenne smile". Smile performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
